Browse to the top level of the chef-repo and enter:

.. code-block:: bash

   $ knife upload roles

or from anywhere in the chef-repo, enter:

.. code-block:: bash

   $ knife upload /roles

to upload all roles that are stored as JSON data. (Roles stored as Ruby data will not be uploaded.)
